Housing Affordability: % of households paying more than 30% of income on housing related experiences (% burdened households)

Data:

	ACS Table DP04 (2010-2014) -- Selected Housing Characteristics 
	http://factfinder.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=ACS_14_5YR_DP04&prodType=table
	housing_affordability_x.csv

	abeed2f8242a2f5fc4f5b8c1ff0ad4a49dd1b76a

Methodology:

"Burdened" households: households paying more than 30% of their income on housing related expenses, both renters and owners; defined by HUD

Percent Burdened Households = 

((# of owner occupied households w/ mortgage  + # of owner occupied households w/o mortgage + # of renters) paying more than 30% of income on housing) / total number of households
