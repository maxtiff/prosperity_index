1. fip_id.xlsx

the "Base" sheet of this dataset contains the names of the 3,143 census counties (from 2010) along with their respective ID. I use this file at the end to
complete the final merge of the higher educational attainment rate across the various years (and files) into one final dataset. 

Link: http://www2.census.gov/geo/docs/reference/codes/files/national_county.txt

2. educational_attainment_yy.csv

This datafile contains the percent of the population with associates degree, bachelors degree, and graduate degree (plus a lot of other variables that we dont use)

2009-2014 5-year ACS estimates, found in table S1501. 

http://factfinder.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=ACS_14_5YR_S1501&prodType=table
